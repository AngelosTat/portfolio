Mickey Mouse Peanuts

Mickey Mouse Peanuts is a game on Roblox made by Katastro5. The game's goal is to find the hidden badges all over the map and, more specifically, to have fun! Playing with your friends will make this game much funnier. I made this with the knowledge of making a game that you won't get addicted to, like you can play this game for a maximum 5 minutes, and with the knowledge of not making a game pay to win. That's why we are selling only one thing, and that's pretty cheap. The only way we are getting financial support is through donations.
